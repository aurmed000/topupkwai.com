How Topupgim Referral Works

The way the Topupgim Referral program works is quite simple. You just join , promote the product, and you can get a commission if there is a transaction through your referral link. Here's how to join the Topupgim Affiliate program.

  1. Create an account at Topupgim
  2. Create a referral link from the product you want to distribute
  3. Share the link to your friends via social media such as YouTube, WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, and others.
  4. If there is a transaction through the link you share, you will get a commission.

 

Commission Amount Per Transaction

The commission you get is 50% of the transaction profit. For example, 284 Mobile Legends Diamonds profit Rp10,000, then you will get a commission of Rp. 5000 .

 

How Long Does It Take For Transaction Links To Be Recorded As Commission?

If someone clicks on your referral link, you will get a commission from all transactions they make for 90 days.

Example: there are 100 people who click on your referral link, and in 3 months their number of transactions is 10 transactions, so if you average it per transaction you can get a commission of IDR 2,000.

100 (people) x 10 (transactions) x Rp. 2000 (commission) = Rp. 2000,000

 

I share Telkomsel credit, but if they buy Axis credit, do I get a commission?

All transactions originating from the referral link you share will be counted as commission, even if they buy a different product. Your Referral Link will stick for 90 Days in their account.
